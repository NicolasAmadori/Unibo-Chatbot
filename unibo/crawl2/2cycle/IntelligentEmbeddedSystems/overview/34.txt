Overview
========

*   Place of teaching Cesena, Forlì
    
*   Language English
    
*   Type of access Open access with assessment of personal competencies
    
*   Fees [Amounts and exemptions](https://corsi.unibo.it/2cycle/IntelligentEmbeddedSystems/fees-amounts-and-exemptions)
    
*   Learning activities [Course structure diagrams](https://corsi.unibo.it/2cycle/IntelligentEmbeddedSystems/course-structure-diagram)
    

The Intelligent Embedded Systems curriculum’s focus is on the enabling technologies, design methodologies, and implementation strategies of intelligent cyber-physical systems, where computation, networking, and physical processes meet and get integrated. There are two tracks available:  
EIT Digital Master School track - in which the first year is attended in the University of Bologna and the second one in another partner university;  
Local track - in which both years can be attended in the University of Bologna  
The curriculum empowers students to become world-class innovators and specialists in the field of embedded systems.

*   [How to enrol](https://corsi.unibo.it/2cycle/IntelligentEmbeddedSystems/how-to-enrol)

5 reasons to enrol on the degree programme
------------------------------------------

Smart embedded technologies are one of the key drivers of the digital transformation

Combine technical experience on embedded systems with management skills

Choose from an international and a local track

Refine the skills related to cyber-physical systems and internet of things, covering a wide range of industries

Practice what you learn with laboratories and activities
