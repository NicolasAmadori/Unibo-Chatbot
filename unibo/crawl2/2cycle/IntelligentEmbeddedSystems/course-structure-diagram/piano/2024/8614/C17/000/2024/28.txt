This curriculum offers two tracks: one local and one in partnership with EIT Digital Master School: https://masterschool.eitdigital.eu/embedded-systems  
The study plans has some differences between the two tracks that are highlighted below. Additionally, students of the EIT track spend the second here in a partner University abroad.
