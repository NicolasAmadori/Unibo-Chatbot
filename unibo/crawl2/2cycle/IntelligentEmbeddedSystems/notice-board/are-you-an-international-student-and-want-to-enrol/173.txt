During the Virtual Fair - Master's on 29 February you will find all the information you need to study in Italy, particularly:

*   General information about the admission and enrolment process 
*   Pre-enrolment and immigration procedures if you are a non-EU student 
*   Study grants and subsidies 
*   Services and opportunities available to you during your studies. 

[Register now](https://open.unibo.it/events/virtual-fair-2024-masters) for the Virtual Fair, you have until 28 February.

**Preview access begins on 21 February**, you can view the programme and choose the events to attend.
