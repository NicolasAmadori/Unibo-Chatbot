Cesena Campus closure for next holidays: 25th, 26th of April and 1st of May 2024 — Intelligent Embedded Systems - Curriculum - Cesena
================================================================================

Because of national holidays, the Cesena Campus will be closed for 3 days

The Cesena Campus will be closed on the 25th of April because of Italian national holiday (festa della Liberazione).

It will be also closed on Friday, the 26th of April, as well as on the 1st of May because of the International Workers' Day.
