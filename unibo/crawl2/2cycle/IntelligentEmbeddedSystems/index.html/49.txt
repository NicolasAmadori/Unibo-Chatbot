Notice Board

### [Competition for prizes within the 2024 Safety Week](https://centri.unibo.it/tutela-promozione-salute-sicurezza/it/agenda/bando-di-concorso-per-2-premi-nell-ambito-della-settimana-della-sicurezza-2024)

### [PNRR PhD Scholarships - 2024 Call for Applications Open](https://www.unibo.it/en/study/phd-professional-masters-specialisation-schools-and-other-programmes/phd/phd-scholarships-over-140-professional-training-development-opportunities)

If you already have a degree or will graduate by the end of October, you can choose your PhD program and apply by August 7.

### [ER.GO (Regional Authority for the Right to Higher Education) study grants - the call for applications is online](https://www.unibo.it/en/news-and-events/notice-board/er-go-regional-authority-for-the-right-to-higher-education-study-grants-the-call-for-applications-is-online)

The Region bears the cost of your academic studies. Submit your application for scholarships, accommodation, and other benefits.

### [AlmaCert call for applications - grant for international language certificates](https://bandi.unibo.it/s/cla/bando-almacert_2024)

Read the call and submit your application by 13 September.

### [Unibo for Refugees - scholarships](https://corsi.unibo.it/2cycle/IntelligentEmbeddedSystems/notice-board/unibo-for-refugees-scholarships)

Ten scholarships are available for those who have already received an ER.GO, UNICORE, or CRUI scholarship for the academic year 2023/24.
