=====================

The right opportunity during Virtual Fair - Master's to learn more about how you can advance your education or embark on a research career.

Every year you can take part in the call for applications for PhDs in any area: humanities, law, economics, engineering, and sciences. Field studies, seminars, workshops, and study abroad programmes are only a part of what is available for your professional development.

You can learn about all this and much more on **29 February** **from 10:00 to 10:30,** during **Virtual Fair - Master's**.

This event will be held only in Italian

[Register now](https://open.unibo.it/events/virtual-fair-2024-masters)!
