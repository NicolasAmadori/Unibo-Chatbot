The grants funded by the [Right to Higher Education](https://www.unibo.it/en/international/Studying-abroad/other-opportunities/Grants-for-studying-abroad-funded-by-the-university-departments) are designed to promote activities including the provision of scholarships for learning experiences abroad.

Eligibility and Requirements
----------------------------

Students can apply to the call if they are **graduating** (or **newly-graduated within 6 months**, only for further research aimed at the publication of an article regarding the topic of the thesis) in the following Degree Programmes:

*   **Master Degree in Digital Transformation Management**
*   **Master Degree in Computer Science and Engineering**
*   **Bachelor Degree in Computer Science and Engineering**

Period abroad and study grant amount
------------------------------------

The period abroad must be of **maximum 6 months**. The study grant is a **partial contribution to your expenses abroad.** The amount of each contribution will be determined according to the characteristics of the destination and the duration of the stay, and **will range from a minimum of €1,000.00 to a maximum of €3,100.00.**

Scholarships for research abroad are incompatible with other scholarships if they are related to the same period of mobility. It is also not possible to benefit of consecutive periods of mobility if they have the same purpose.

Recognition of the mobility period abroad
-----------------------------------------

Winners will be required to submit to the Teaching Office (Ufficio gestione C.d.S. Ingegneria, Scienze e Architettura, via dell'Università n. 50, Cesena - (email: [campuscesena.internazionalizzazioneingegneria@unibo.it](mailto:campuscesena.internazionalizzazioneingegneria@unibo.it)) all the documents mentioned in the section of the Call for Applications "Obligations following the award" under the terms and procedures provided.

The learned activity carried out abroad will be recognized, at the end of the mobility period, only for those who have submitted such documentation.

Deadlines and Call of applications
----------------------------------

The deadline to submit your application is: **March 13th 2024 at 1.00 PM (CEST)**  
For further information check the University portal [Bandi, Gare e Concorsi](https://bandi.unibo.it/s/campus-cesena/bando-tesi-all-estero-disi-febbraio-2024_campus-di-cesena) for the complete documentation.
