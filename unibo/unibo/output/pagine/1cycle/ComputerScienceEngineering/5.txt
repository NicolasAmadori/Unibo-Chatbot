*   Place of teaching Cesena
    
*   Type of access Restricted access
    
*   Degree Programme Class L-8 - Information technology engineering

*   Degree Programme Director [Franco Callegati](https://www.unibo.it/uniboweb/Utils/sitowebpersonale.aspx?MAT=30946&lang=en)
    
*   Learning activities [Course structure diagram](https://corsi.unibo.it/1cycle/ComputerScienceEngineering/course-structure-diagram)
    

Programme aims
--------------

Design, develop and manage systems and applications in the field of IT, study the fundamentals of science and engineering with in-depth knowledge of design techniques and methodologies and the most up-to-date tools for implementing IT solutions.
